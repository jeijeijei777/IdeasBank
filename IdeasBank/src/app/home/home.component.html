What is Trust

Trust is a service that allows you to evaluate the competencies of others on the blockchain and conduct various recruitment.

Recruitment is not limited to personnel; it can also be used to place and accept business orders.


The competencies of others are evaluated between 0 ~ 1 and the cumulative point is the individual's behavioral characteristics score.

In addition to the core competencies prepared from the beginning, it is also possible to display unique behavioral characteristics by applying to us.

IdeasBankとは

IdeasBankは、ブロックチェーン上で皆様のアイデアを安全に共有できるサービスです。

世界を変える独自のアイデアを、IdeasBankではいくつも見つけることが出来ます。


他者のアイデアを0〜1の間で評価し、その累計ポイントが個人のアイデアレベルになります。
